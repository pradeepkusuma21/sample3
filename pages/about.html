About Infopath Solutions

Empowering businesses and careers through exceptional IT talent solutions

Infopath Solutions specializes in information technology staffing and training for companies across the United States. We provide comprehensive services including IT staffing, consulting, application development, business intelligence, Cloud (AWS/Azure/GCP), and professional training programs.

We are experts in staffing for all phases of the project lifecycle including requirements management, business analysis, architecture, application development & maintenance, quality assurance testing, project management, desktop support, and network and server infrastructure support and maintenance.

Our company was founded on the principles of customer first, honesty, and employee development, and we remain committed to these ideals. We are dedicated to supplying the right candidates and providing a superior level of service to offer the best return on investment to our clients.

Our Mission

To connect exceptional IT talent with leading organizations, fostering growth and innovation. We strive to be the most trusted partner in IT recruitment and training, delivering value through rigorous screening, personalized service, and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our Rigorous Screening Process

We follow a comprehensive three-level screening method for every candidate

1

Aptitude Screening

Comprehensive assessment of problem-solving abilities, logical reasoning, and cognitive skills to ensure candidates can handle complex technical challenges.

2

Personality Screening

Evaluation of cultural fit, communication skills, teamwork capabilities, and professional demeanor to ensure seamless integration with your team.

3

Technical Screening

In-depth technical interviews and hands-on assessments customized for each client's specific requirements, ensuring candidates possess the exact skills needed.

Our goal: Ensure candidates get the work done perfectly, fit well into your team, and add real value. Our high acceptance ratio means you spend less time narrowing down candidates.
